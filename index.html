Equipment lending

Borrow equipment from any UT Libraries branch with your UT EID. Items check out for 3 days and can be renewed once.

Borrowing policies
Loan period: 3 days, renewable once
Valid UT EID required to borrow
Return to any UTL branch that carries the item
Lost or damaged items may incur replacement fees
Renewals available online or at a service desk
Items with holds cannot be renewed
